Gas comes prefilled as well. Then we will drive towards the Sydney Opera House. Once we are done visiting the Sydney Opera House, we will go to BridgeClimb Sydney. This touist attraction guides to tourists to climbing the Sydney Harbour Bridge. The price to book a climb is $442 dollars. After that, we will go the Sea Life Sydney Aquarium where we can see beautiful aquatic wild life. A ticket for two adult cost $102 dollars.
